Our Training Process

Each service animal undergoes an intensive 12-18 month training program tailored to veteran needs:

Basic Obedience

4-6 months of foundational training in commands and public behavior

Specialized Skills

6-8 months learning disability-specific tasks

Team Training

2-4 months matching with veteran and joint training

Finding Your Perfect Match

Choosing the right service animal is crucial for success. Consider these factors:

  • Your specific physical or emotional needs
  • Living environment and activity level
  • Allergies or breed preferences
  • Long-term commitment to animal care

Our staff will help you through every step of the matching process.
