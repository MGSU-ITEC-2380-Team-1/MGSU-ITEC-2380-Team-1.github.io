Meet Our Service Dogs

Buddy is three years old. He has been trained with associated professionals to assist in mobility assistance amd emotional support. He is licensed through the National Therapist Program, and recieved his license on October 5th, 2024.

Luna is four years old. She has been trained to be aware of upcoming PTSD attacks and "alert" to them. This signals for the owner to make the proper precautions when going through ther daily life. She is licensed through the National Therapist Program, and recieved his license on June 25th, 2024.

Max is six years old. He has been trained to be a comfort to people who struggle with their emotional regulation after their service. He is licensed through the National Therapist Program, and received his license on April 16th, 2023.

Choosing the Right Service Animal

Finding the best companion depends on your needs and lifestyle.

  • Consider your daily routine and physical activity level.
  • Identify specific tasks the animal should be trained for.
  • Ensure compatibility with your home environment and lifestyle.
  • Work with a certified trainer or organization to match appropriately.
