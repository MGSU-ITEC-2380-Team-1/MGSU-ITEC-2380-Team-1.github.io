Visit Our Training Facility

Our 5-acre facility includes indoor training spaces, outdoor obstacle courses, and comfortable meeting areas where veterans can get to know potential service animals.

Parking: Free parking is available in front of our building. Additional accessible parking spaces are located near the entrance.

Volunteer Opportunities

Animal Care

Help with feeding, grooming, and basic training of our service animals in training.

  • 2-4 hour shifts available
  • No experience required
  • Training provided

Veteran Support

Assist veterans during training sessions and help with facility tours.

  • Background check required
  • Military experience preferred
  • Weekday availability needed

Event Staff

Help organize and run fundraising events and community outreach programs.

  • Flexible scheduling
  • Great for groups
  • Various roles available

Professional Skills

  • Project-based opportunities
  • Remote work possible
  • Portfolio building
