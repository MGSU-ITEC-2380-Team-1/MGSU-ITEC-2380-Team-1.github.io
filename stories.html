Sean & Pella: A New Lease on Life

"Pella makes it possible for me to face crowds, to shop in a busy supermarket and eat in a restaurant without anxiety."

Major Sean Brown, Army Veteran

After serving two tours in Afghanistan, Sean returned home with severe PTSD that made everyday activities nearly impossible. Crowds triggered panic attacks, and sleepless nights became the norm.

Pella, a Labrador Retriever trained specifically for PTSD support, changed everything. She senses Sean's rising anxiety before he does, nudging him to sit down or move to a quieter space. At night, she interrupts nightmares by gently licking his face.

"She's not just a dog - she's my battle buddy for this new fight," Sean says. "With Pella by my side, I've been able to return to college and even speak at veteran events about PTSD awareness."
