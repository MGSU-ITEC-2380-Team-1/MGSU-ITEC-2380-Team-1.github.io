"Before Max, I barely left the house. Now, I go to the grocery store without fear. He's more than a dog — he's my freedom. "

Sergeant Marcus Taylor, USMC (Ret.)

"Marcus served two tours overseas and struggled with PTSD after returning home. When he was paired with Max, a dog trained in anxiety interruption, his life began to turn around. Max senses Marcus’ anxiety before it escalates, grounding him with touch and presence.

"Luna doesn't just guide me — she gives me the confidence to live fully again."

Corporal Jennifer Lee, Army Veteran

After losing her vision due to combat injuries, Jennifer felt isolated. Luna, a dog trained in mobility and visual guidance, brought purpose and independence back into Jennifer's daily life. Their bond is unbreakable.

“Pella makes it possible for me to face crowds, to shop in a busy supermarket and eat in a restaurant without anxiety. Far from being a pet, she provides me steady empathy, support and sensitivity to the flashbacks, nightmares and stress that used to be crippling.”

Major Sean Brown, Army Veteran

Sean's loyalty to our country left him with PTSD induced anxiety. Pella, a dog trained to help with anxiety induced PTSD, brought a sense of calmness to Sean.
