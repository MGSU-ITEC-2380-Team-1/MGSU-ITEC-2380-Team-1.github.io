Adoption Process FAQs

The entire process typically takes 3-6 months from application to placement. This includes application review, interviews, home assessment, matching, and training.

While we provide the service animal at no cost, adopters are responsible for ongoing care including food, veterinary expenses, and equipment. We can provide resources for financial assistance if needed.

Our adoption contract requires that if you can no longer care for the animal, it must be returned to our organization. We will work to find an appropriate new placement.
